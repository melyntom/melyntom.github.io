About Me

Detail-driven and creatively curious, I thrive at the intersection of data, technology, and design.

I’m a detail-obsessed professional who loves turning chaos into clarity, whether it’s organizing 26,000+ SKUs in a fashion catalog, pulling insights from data, or coordinating all the moving parts of an unforgettable experience.

With a B.S. in Information Systems and Technology from CSU San Bernardino, I’ve built a strong foundation in data analysis, digital forensics, and project management. I also helped produce instructional content for faculty, bringing tech and storytelling together to make complex topics click.

I thrive where systems meet creativity, whether that’s in the fast-paced world of fashion e-commerce, the strategy-driven space of analytics, or the graceful chaos of event planning. I bring agile problem-solving, collaborative teamwork, and a positive, can-do attitude to every team I join.
